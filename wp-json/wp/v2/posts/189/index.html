According to Lencioni, an Ideal Team player is someone who <\/p>\n\n\n\n

  • Is Humble, Hungry and Smart (people)<\/em><\/li>
  • Is someone who can easily build Trust<\/li>
  • Has healthy conflict
    • “Argues about the right way to get things done without worrying that they’re going to offend someone”<\/em><\/li><\/ul><\/li>
    • is Capable of being honest & vulnerable
      • “Getting people to admit when they make a mistake on a project”<\/em><\/li><\/ul><\/li>
      • Holds each other in the team accountable<\/li>
      • is Results oriented (team’s results) , history of getting difficult things done.
        • “Sticking to commitments”<\/em><\/li><\/ul><\/li><\/ul>\n\n\n\n
